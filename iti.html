CHAR DHAM TOUR

Day 1

Arrival and Evening Aarti

Arrive in the holy city of Haridwar or Rishikesh and check into your hotel or guesthouse. Spend the evening attending the Ganga Aarti on the ghats of the River Ganges, a spiritual and mesmerizing experience.

Day 2

Barkot - Yamunotri - Barkot (Drive and Trek)

Depart early for Janki Chatti, the starting point of your trek to Yamunotri. Trek to Yamunotri, a distance of around 6 km, or you can hire a horse or palanquin. Take a holy dip in the Yamuna River and visit the Yamunotri Temple. Return to Barkot in the evening.

Day 3

Barkot - Uttarkashi (Drive)

Drive to Uttarkashi, a charming town on the banks of the Bhagirathi River.Visit the famous Vishwanath Temple and other local attractions.Check into your accommodation in Uttarkashi.

Day 4

Uttarkashi - Gangotri - Uttarkashi (Drive)

Depart early for Gangotri, one of the Char Dham sites. Visit the Gangotri Temple and offer your prayers. Return to Uttarkashi in the evening..
